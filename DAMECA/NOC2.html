NOC of Issuance and Renewal of Licenses for Maritime Companies and Establishments Operating in the Special Development Zones

In addition to any requirements for issuance or renewal of a license required by Trakhees, the applicant must submit to DMCA a business plan for its review and approval. Upon review and approval of the business plan, DMCA shall issue a No Objection Certificate to the applicant within 3 working days.

Requirements:

(a)        New Application

  1. Business plan
  2. Initial approval (copy) issued by Trakhees
  3. Supporting documents to prove expertise and competence in the maritime activity(ies) sought for issuance of license
  4. If carrying out the maritime activity(ies) require the use of vessel(s), inspection of the vessel(s) must also be carried out by DMCA.

(b)        Renewal

  1. Current license (copy);
  2. Supporting documents to prove expertise and competence in the maritime activity(ies) as stated in the current license
  3. If carrying out the maritime activity(ies) require the use of vessel(s), inspection of the vessel(s) must also be carried out by DMCA.
