NOC of Issuance and Renewal of Licenses for Maritime Companies and Establishments Operating in the Emirate of Dubai

In addition to any requirements for issuance of a license required by the Dubai Department of Economic Development, the applicant must submit to DMCA:

(a)        New Application

  1. Initial approval (copy) issued by Dubai Department of Economic Development;
  2. Supporting documents to prove expertise and competence in the maritime activity(ies) sought for issuance of license
  3. If carrying out the maritime activity(ies) require the use of vessel(s), inspection of the vessel(s) must also be carried out by DMCA.

(b)        Renewal

  1. Current license (copy);
  2. Supporting documents to prove expertise and competence in the maritime activity(ies) as stated in the current license
  3. If carrying out the maritime activity(ies) require the use of vessel(s), inspection of the vessel(s) must also be carried out by DMCA.
