Requirements

(A) Marine Craft Driving License:

1. Equation of Marine Craft Driving License certificates

To obtain any type of Marine Craft driving license, the Authority requires submitting a training certificate from a certified training institute, authorizing the applicant to drive the same type of Marine Craft. It is also possible to wave this condition if the applicant submits any proof of driving a marine craft experience for not less than one year on the Emirates. In addition the applicant should pass the test conducted by the Authority to drive a pleasure marine craft up to (12) meters.   

For the applicant who has an international foreign driving license or certificates from uncertified training institute by the Authority, the applicant should apply for Certificate Equation & submit all the required documents to be reviewed by the Authority to assure that it matched with its certified rules & regulations.

Required documents:

  1. Filling the Marine Crew Licensing Application form (Ref. No. LR-FRM-004-V1)
  2. Passport, UAE family book or Emirates Identity card copy for UAE National, or Passport, UAE valid visa or Emirates identity card coy for expects.
  3. One recent Personal Photograph with white color background.
  4. The original Marine craft driving certificate.
  5. The applicant’s age should not less than 16 years.
  6. NOC from sponsor for non-UAE national
  7. Medical fitness certificate from approved medical centers.

(C) Marine Crew Permit:
1. Issuing a Marin Pilot permits

Required documents:

  1. Filling the Marine Crew Licensing Application form (Ref. No. LR-FRM-004-V1)
  2. One recent Personal Photograph with white color background.
  3. Passport, UAE family book or Emirates Identity card copy for UAE National, or Passport, UAE valid visa or Emirates identity card coy for expects.
  4. The applicant’s age shouldn’t be less than 25 years.
  5. Copy of the skipper high seas certificate.
  6. Copy of an experience certificate for not less than two years as a marine Pilot or passing the training program conducted by the employer for not less than three months according to the standards specified by the (IMO).
  7. A letter forwarded to the authority from the employer  indicating that the applicant is working with them as a marine pilot.
  8. Medical fitness certificate issued by a medical center approved by the authority.
  9. Certificate of good conduct issued by Dubai Police General Headquarters.
2. Issuing a Marine Trainer

Required documents:

  1. Filling in a marine crew licensing form (form number LA-FRM-004-VI)
  2. 1 colored photo with a white background.
  3. A copy of the family book and the passport or the Emirati ID card as for the citizens, or a copy of the passport and valid residence permit or Emirati ID card as for non citizens.
  4. A letter forwarded to the authority from the employer indicating that the applicant is working with them as a marine trainer.
  5. Submitting prove documents of  taking the basic safety courses for sea  workers including the skills of using safety equipments, rescue, first aids and public safety, firefighting and preventing marine pollution. 
  6. Marine or professional certificates (if any). 
  7. Medical fitness certificate issued by a medical center approved by the authority.
  8. Certificate of good conduct issued by Dubai Police General Headquarters.
3. Renewing the license of Marine Craft crew

Required documents:

  1. Filling in a marine crew licensing form (form number LA-FRM-004-VI)
  2. 1 colored photo with a white background.
  3. The original copy of the Marine Permit.
  4. A copy of the family book and the passport or the Emirati ID card as for the citizens, or a copy of the passport and valid residence permit or Emirati ID card as for non citizens.
  5. A copy of the expired certificate of renewing qualifications.
  6. Medical fitness certificate issued by a medical centers approved by the authority.
  7. Certificate of good conduct issued by Dubai Police General Headquarters.
4. Amending the license of the Marine Craft crew

Required documents:

  1. Filling in a Marine Craft licensing form (form number LA-FRM-004-VI)
  2. 1 colored photo with a white background.
  3.  A copy of the family book and the passport or the Emirati ID card as for the citizens, or a copy of the passport and valid residence permit or Emirati ID card as for non citizens.
  4. The original copy of the Marine Permit.
  5. In case of amending the personal details of the license holder, then it should amend all documents in this regard.
  6. If the amendment requires to upgrade the license category from the lowest category to the highest category, or to amend the type of license, from recreational to commercial, the applicant should submit all documents required for issuing a new license according to the procedures and condition applied by the authority.  
5. Issuing a replacement of a damaged or lost license of a marine crew

Required documents:

  1. Filling in a marine crew licensing form (form number LA-FRM-004-VI)
  2. 1 colored photo with a white background.
  3.  A copy of the family book and the passport or the Emirati ID card as for the citizens, or a copy of the passport and valid residence permit or Emirati ID card as for non citizens.
  4. The original copy of the Marine Permit in case of damaging
  5. A notification issued by Dubai Police general Headquarters to prove losing the license.
