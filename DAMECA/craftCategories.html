Marine Craft Categories:

(1) Pleasure Marine Craft

Pleasure Marine Craft with engine such as:

  • Personal Water Craft (Jet skis)
  • Pleasured Marine Craft up to 12m
  • Pleasured Marine Craft between 12m to less than 24 m
  • Pleasured Marine Craft over 24m

Pleasure Marine Craft without engine such as:

  • Rowing Marine Craft
  • Sailing Marine Craft

(2) Traditional Wooden Crafts

such as:

Abras, wooden floating restaurants, wooden pleasure boats and what comes in its condition.

(3) Commercial Marine Craft

These are the Marine Craft allocated for commercial business such as:

  • Pleasure Marine Craft for chartering
  • Marine Craft operating in the ports
  • Marine Craft used for Construction
  • Marine Craft used for Towing & Pilotage
  • Marine Craft used for Public transportation
  • Marine Craft used for Cargo transportation
