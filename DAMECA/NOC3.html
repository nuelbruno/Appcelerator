NOC of Renewal and Amendment of Licenses for Maritime Companies and Establishments Operating in the Jebel Ali Free Zone

In addition to any requirements for issuance of a license required by Jebel Ali Free Zone Authority, the applicant must submit to DMCA:

(a)        Renewal

  1. Letter from applicant (authorized signatory) to renew the license;
  2. Current license (copy) (JAFZA and DED, if applicable);
  3. Supporting documents to prove expertise and competence in the maritime activity(ies) as stated in the current license; and
  4. If carrying out the maritime activity(ies) require the use of vessel(s), inspection of the vessel(s) must also be carried out by DMCA.

(b)        Amendment


  1. Letter from applicant (authorized signatory) to request for an amendment to the license;
  2. Current license (copy) (JAFZA and DED, if applicable);
  3. Supporting documents to prove expertise and competence in the maritime activity(ies) as stated in the current license; and
  4. If carrying out the maritime activity(ies) require the use of vessel(s), inspection of the vessel(s) must also be carried out by DMCA.
