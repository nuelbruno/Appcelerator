NOC of Issuance and Renewal of Licenses for floating restaurants in Dubai

Dubai Maritime City authority is responsible for floating restaurants activity in Dubai, therefore the client has to obtain DMCA approval in order to complete the new issuance / renewal of the license with DED.

In order to obtain approval DMCA inspector will inspect the floating restaurant, and if the floating restaurant comply with all the safety rolls and regulation requirements, DMCA will provide approval online on the DED system.
