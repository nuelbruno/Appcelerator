Requirements
Issuance of Marine Craft License

Required Documents :
1. Fill a Marine Craft licensing application form (Ref No.: LR-FRM-001-V1)
2. Original manufacturing certificate of the new Marine Craft
3. Valid technical inspection passing certificate for the marine Craft issued by DMCA or the authority accredited thereby
4. A copy of the title deed of the Marine Craft.
5. A copy of the Marine Craft cancellation of registration in the last entity where it was registered if it was used, and a proof that the Marine Craft is free of any mortgage.
6. The original copy of the customs clearance certificate issued by the competent authorities if the Marine Craft is imported
7. The original copy of the bill of sale for local purchase of the Marine Craft attested by the competent authorities.
8. Passport, UAE family book and Emirates Identity card copy for UAE National, or Passport, UAE valid visa and Emirates Identity card coy for expects.
9. A copy of the valid trade license if the owner of the Marine Craft is a corporate
10. The original copy of the insurance policy for the commercial marine Craft, traditional wooden Crafts, visitor Marine Craft and mortgaged marine craft, valid for thirteen (13) months, issued by an insurance firm licensed to operate in the State, or a compulsory insurance policy for the pleasured Marine Craft, valid for thirteen (13) months and issued by an insurance firm licensed to operate in the State
11. The original copy of purchasing certificate of the (VHF) device from a certified supplier for the Marine Craft over (35) ft.
12. A copy of the mortgage contract for the mortgaged Marine Craft concluded between the owner of the Marine Craft and the mortgagee
13. Stability booklet for the commercial Marine Craft less than or over (24) m. or those licensed to carry over 12 passengers
14. Classification Authority Certificate for the commercial Marine Craft less than or over (24) m. or 150 GT or above.
15. Installing a GPS Tracker for the Marine Craft less than 300 GT as per the requirements of the Coast Guards

In case the Craft is allocated for passenger transportation, the following Documents shall be submitted:
1. An NOC to operate the Marine Craft, issued by the Road and Transport Authority ( RTA)
2. Submit an acknowledgement on the Marine Craft journey and its service timings, as well as its approved speeds issued by the Road and Transport Authority ( RTA)
