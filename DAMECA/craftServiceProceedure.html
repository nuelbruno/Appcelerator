Service Procedures
  1. Fill a Marine Craft licensing application form (Ref No.: LR-FRM-001-V1)
  2. Submit all the required documents to issue Marine Craft  Licensing
  3. Submit the Marine Craft Inspection fees
  4. Reserve the name & the Number of the Marine Craft
  5. Install the Marine Craft Number & complete the Inspection process.  
  6. Proceed with the Marine Craft registration process and pay the applicable fees
  7. Receive the Marine Craft License & the Minimum safe manning certificate ( for commercial Marine Crafts only )
  8. Install the GPS Tracker belongs to the Coastguard
  9. Install the VHF device for commercial & pleasure marine craft more than 36 Ft.
